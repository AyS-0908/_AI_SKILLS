# Nestor Reflection - User Guide

Use this Skill when you need a structured reflection that ends with a decision-ready report.

## Before the first reflection

Create the six neutral Google templates once. Follow [google-workspace.md](google-workspace.md#one-time-template-setup). Use a Mac for this one-time setup; the generated Google files can then be used on iPhone or iPad.

## 1. Start a reflection

Say:

```text
Run a Nestor reflection on: <topic>.
Context: <what happened or why this matters>.
Expected decision: <what I need to decide>.
Attachments: <links or files, if any>.
```

The AI summarizes its understanding, gives an initial opinion, recommends a method, and asks only the missing scope questions. Reply with the numbered answers, then say `VALIDATE SCOPE`.

## 2. Choose the method

| Method | Use it when | Main output |
|---|---|---|
| Interviews | You need comparable views from people | Questions, Form or interview notes, analysis |
| Data research | You need evidence from reliable external sources | Source plan, evidence table, analysis |
| Brainstorming | You need options and a structured choice | Ideas, clusters, scoring, analysis |

Answer the method questions, give the target Google Drive folder, then say `VALIDATE METHOD`.

## 3. Review the working material

The AI creates or copies the right Google Docs and Sheets. Open the links, edit the questions, audience, sources, ideas, or assumptions, then validate in the chat.

For an interview Survey, say `VALIDATE QUESTIONNAIRE` only when the exact questions, invitation text, names, and email addresses are ready. The AI creates the Form and Gmail drafts; it never sends emails.

## 4. Review the analysis and report

The AI creates an analysis Sheet and a concise pre-report. It also asks a fresh AI reviewer to challenge weak logic, unsupported conclusions, and missing alternatives.

Correct anything needed, then say `VALIDATE PRE-REPORT`. The AI creates the final report only from your approved branded template.

## Resume a reflection

Say:

```text
Resume Nestor reflection: <name>.
Stage: <last known stage>.
Links: <Google file links>.
```

The AI verifies the last approval before continuing. If approval or a file is unclear, it stops and asks you to reconcile it.

## Important rules

- The AI asks for approval before moving to the next major stage.
- Research conclusions must trace to accepted sources; opinions are labelled as judgment.
- The AI creates email drafts only; you decide whether to send them.
- Do not delete or rename working files during the reflection; share their updated links instead.
