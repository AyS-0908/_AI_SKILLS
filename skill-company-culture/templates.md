## Lightweight Rollout

```txt
Outcome:
Audience:
Owner:

Define:
- Confirm evidence, principles, behaviours, and trade-offs.

Manager pilot:
- Test wording and practical use with a small manager group.
- Remove anything managers cannot apply.

Rollout:
- Explain what changes in everyday decisions and behaviour.
- Use existing meetings and channels.

Sustain:
- Reinforce through relevant people processes.
- Review observable signals and decide what to adjust.
```

## Workshop Agenda

```txt
Objective:
Define the few behaviours Dapple must preserve or change as it grows.

Agenda:
1. What business outcome should culture support?
2. Which current behaviours help or hurt execution?
3. What do visible artifacts reveal?
4. Which stated values match or conflict with reality?
5. Which assumptions drive repeated decisions?
6. Draft 3-5 principles with behaviours and trade-offs.
7. Decide owner, manager pilot, and next review.
```
