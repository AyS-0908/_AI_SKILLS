# Company Culture References

Sources checked on 2026-07-17. Use them as evidence and adaptation guides, not as models to copy. Verify official guidance live before country-specific legal or policy use.

## Core Sources

| Source | Type | What to use |
|---|---|---|
| [Gallup: Diagnosing a Broken Culture](https://www.gallup.com/workplace/509069/diagnosing-broken-culture.aspx) | Practitioner research | Define, align, drive, and sustain culture; start from current strengths and tensions; connect behaviour to leaders, managers, systems, and rituals. |
| [Gallup: Sustain Culture as You Scale](https://www.gallup.com/workplace/353579/organization-sustain-culture-scale.aspx) | Practitioner research | Treat managers as culture transmitters and prevent inconsistent decisions as layers and locations grow. |
| [MIT Sloan: Edgar Schein's enduring management ideas](https://mitsloan.mit.edu/ideas-made-to-matter/5-enduring-management-ideas-mit-sloans-edgar-schein) | Official university summary | Diagnose artifacts, espoused values, and underlying assumptions; use careful inquiry rather than taking stated values at face value. |
| [Atlassian Team Playbook](https://www.atlassian.com/team-playbook/plays) | Primary practitioner guidance | Select only the lightweight play needed for roles, decision ownership, working agreements, retrospectives, or OKRs. |
| [CIPD: Organisational climate and culture](https://www.cipd.org/uk/knowledge/factsheets/organisation-culture-change-factsheet/) | Professional-body guidance | Turn broad culture language into specific employee experiences, policies, practices, and observable behaviours. |
| [CIPD: Workforce planning](https://www.cipd.org/en/knowledge/factsheets/workforce-planning-factsheet/) | Professional-body guidance | Adapt practices to the organisation's size and maturity and link them to business needs and observable triggers. |

## Employer-Foundation Boundary

Culture work does not replace employer basics.

| Location | Source | Boundary |
|---|---|---|
| United States | [SBA: Hire and manage employees](https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/manage-your-business/hire-manage-employees) | Payroll, classification, records, benefits, and federal/state duties remain separate from culture choices. |
| United States | [U.S. Department of Labor: New and Small Businesses](https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/compliance-assistance/small-business) | Wages, hours, notices, recordkeeping, and state requirements need current checks. |
| United Kingdom | [GOV.UK: Employ people](https://www.gov.uk/browse/business/employ-people) | Contracts, payroll, pensions, leave, workplace duties, and exits cannot be inferred from values. |
| France | [Urssaf: Embaucher un salarie](https://www.urssaf.fr/accueil/embaucher-gerer-salarie/employeur/embaucher-salarie.html) | Hiring declarations, payroll, and social contributions remain formal employer requirements. |

## Adaptation Rules

- Start from observed behaviour and real decisions, not a famous company's value list.
- Preserve useful existing culture before designing an aspirational one.
- Prefer 3-5 principles with examples, anti-behaviours, and trade-offs.
- Use existing management routines before adding culture programs.
- Add measurement only when a decision or course correction will follow.
- Apply **Needed now / Add when / Not needed yet** from approximately 50 to 250 employees.
