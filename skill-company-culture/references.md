# Company Culture References

Sources checked on 2026-07-16. Use them as evidence and adaptation guides, not as models to copy.

## Core Sources

| Source | Type | What To Use |
|---|---|---|
| [Gallup: Diagnosing a Broken Culture](https://www.gallup.com/workplace/509069/diagnosing-broken-culture.aspx) | Practitioner research | Define, align, drive, and sustain culture; connect it to strategy, employee experience, managers, systems, and rituals. |
| [MIT Sloan: Edgar Schein's enduring management ideas](https://mitsloan.mit.edu/ideas-made-to-matter/5-enduring-management-ideas-mit-sloans-edgar-schein) | Official university summary | Diagnose artifacts, stated values, and underlying assumptions; reinforce culture through leadership attention, measures, rewards, hiring, retention, and performance decisions. |
| [Atlassian Team Playbook](https://www.atlassian.com/team-playbook/plays) | Primary practitioner guidance | Select lightweight exercises for roles, decision ownership, working agreements, retrospectives, and OKRs. Use only the play that solves the current problem. |
| [CIPD: Organisational climate and culture](https://www.cipd.org/uk/knowledge/factsheets/organisation-culture-change-factsheet/) | Professional-body guidance | Prefer specific employee experiences, policies, and behaviours over vague culture language. |
| [CIPD: Workforce planning](https://www.cipd.org/en/knowledge/factsheets/workforce-planning-factsheet/) | Professional-body guidance | Adjust people practices to organizational size and maturity; link them to business needs and observable triggers. |

## Employer-Foundation Boundary

These sources prevent culture work from replacing employer basics. Use current live guidance for any legal or country-specific answer.

| Location | Source | Type | Boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| United States | [SBA: Hire and manage employees](https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/manage-your-business/hire-manage-employees) | Official government guidance | Payroll, worker classification, records, benefits, and federal/state duties are separate from culture choices. |
| United States | [U.S. Department of Labor: New and Small Businesses](https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/compliance-assistance/small-business) | Official government guidance | Wage, hours, notices, and recordkeeping require current compliance checks. |
| United Kingdom | [GOV.UK: Employ people](https://www.gov.uk/browse/business/employ-people) and [employment-status checklist](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/employment-status-and-employment-rights/employment-status-and-rights-checklist-for-employers-and-other-engagers) | Official government guidance | Contracts, payroll, pensions, employment status, leave, and workplace duties cannot be inferred from values. |
| France | [Urssaf: Declaration prior to hiring](https://www.urssaf.fr/accueil/employeur/embaucher-gerer-salaries/embaucher/declaration-prealable-embauche.html) | Official public-body guidance | Hiring declarations, payroll, and social obligations remain formal employer requirements. |

## Adaptation Rules

- Start from observed behaviour and real decisions, not a famous company's value list.
- Preserve useful existing culture before designing an aspirational one.
- Prefer 3-5 principles with examples and trade-offs.
- Use existing management routines before adding culture programs.
- Add measurement only when a decision or course correction will follow.
- Apply **Needed now / Add when / Not needed yet** so the method remains useful from 50 to 250 employees.
- Treat laws and official guidance as time-sensitive; verify them live before legal or policy use.
